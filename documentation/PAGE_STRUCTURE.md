# 📂 Page Structure

Explore the wide array of functional pages included in The SoundBox template.

## **Main Home Pages**
- `index.html`: The multi-purpose home page with a high-impact hero section and event highlight components.
- `index-2.html`: A specialized home page version with a distinct layout focusing on artist spotlights and upcoming show lineups.

## **Core User Pages**
- `about.html`: Venue story, mission, and history section.
- `services.html`: Private events, venue rental details, and special service packages.
- `blog.html`: Latest news, music industry updates, and artist interviews.
- `contact.html`: Contact form, Google Maps integration, and social links.
- `shows.html`: Full event calendar with advanced filtering and ticket booking buttons.

## **Authentication Flow**
- `login.html`: Secure user login with Google and Facebook social auth options.
- `signup.html`: Comprehensive signup form with password validation and social auth.

## **User Dashboard**
- `dashboard-user.html`: Main overview of user tickets and loyalty points.
- `dashboard-user-orders.html`: History of purchased concert tickets.
- `dashboard-user-saved.html`: Collection of saved artists and favorite shows.
- `dashboard-user-messages.html`: Notifications and support chat messages.
- `dashboard-user-settings.html`: Personal profile and preference management.

## **Admin Dashboard**
- `dashboard-admin.html`: Overview of venue performance and real-time sales analytics.
- `dashboard-admin-users.html`: User list, role management, and fan group stats.
- `dashboard-admin-content.html`: Tools to manage shows, blog posts, and artist lineups.
- `dashboard-admin-orders.html`: Comprehensive ticket sales and revenue tracking.
- `dashboard-admin-settings.html`: Global venue and platform configuration.

## **Utility Pages**
- `404.html`: Custom error page with a return-to-home button.
- `coming-soon.html`: Countdown timer and newsletter sign-up for upcoming venue launches.
