About The SoundBox

Our History

15 Years of Live Music Excellence

Founded in 2009, The SoundBox began as a small warehouse space with a big dream: to create a venue where artists and audiences could connect on a deeper level.

What started as a passion project by three music-loving friends has grown into one of the city's most beloved live music destinations, hosting over 500 shows per year and welcoming more than 50,000 fans annually.

Our historic building, originally a 1920s textile mill, has been carefully restored and acoustically engineered to provide the perfect setting for unforgettable performances across all genres.
